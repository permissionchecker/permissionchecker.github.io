      We will first encrypt data through **AES256** algorithm, which is currently impossible to break, and transmit it secretly to our server.

      We will only use the data for reseach purposes only. Aggregated information may be shared with third parties and reasonable efforts will be taken to ensure that individuals cannot be re-identified through ordinary means.

      Please contact us at `operarelease@gmail.com`. We will remove all the data collected from your device and will not give it to any third party.
